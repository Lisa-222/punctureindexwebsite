Swarm

Video, 3D animation


This Sci-Fi documentary explores relationships of humans and synthetic creatures where the value of society roles seems to be at risk. The documentary goes through the momentums of everyday life where their importance is being at the brink of automatization. The work offers a retrospective view on the life of the creatures and how they are being influenced by the environment around them. Documentary captures the slow change of the creatures through different environments and how they change their behaviors with it.
